Refund Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

We want you to be happy with Lectern. This policy explains how refunds work for purchases of Lectern Pro from Necropolis Publishing, LLC.

Try the free tier first

Lectern has a free tier that includes the full writing surface, so you can confirm Lectern works on your device and suits the way you write before you ever pay. We encourage you to start there.

30-day money-back guarantee

If Lectern Pro isn't right for you, you may request a full refund within thirty (30) days of your purchase. This applies to the Founder's Edition as well. There's nothing to return, and your license key stays yours — keep Lectern Pro as our gift, with our thanks for giving it a try.

How to request a refund

Email contact@necropolispublishing.com from the address you used to purchase, and include your order or receipt details. We'll refund you to your original payment method.

Timing

Once approved, refunds are processed promptly. The time for funds to appear in your account depends on your bank or card provider, typically a few business days.

Questions

If anything about a purchase or refund is unclear, email contact@necropolispublishing.com and we'll help.
