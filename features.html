Features

A complete writing studio.

Shaped around the way books are actually made — paragraph by paragraph, scene by scene. Everything below ships today.

Free to Begin
☙❧

The whole craft of drafting, organizing, and revising a novel — at no cost.

The Page

Book Mode

Words settle onto real, bordered pages, one after another, with ornamental breaks marking each scene — your manuscript takes the shape of a finished book rather than an endless scroll, so pacing and chapter weight are visible at a glance.

Living Markdown

Headings, bold and italic, blockquotes, lists, and ornamental dividers render as you type — while the formatting marks themselves slip out of sight until your cursor returns to the line.

Raw View

A single keystroke reveals the clean markdown beneath the page — the same plain text saved to disk — and returns you to the book exactly where you were.

Typewriter Calm

The line you're writing holds steady at a height you choose while the page rises to meet it, so your eyes rest in one place through hours at the desk.

Tranquility Mode

One keystroke clears the room: shelf, outline, and every panel fall away until nothing remains but the page and the words on it. A brush of the cursor at the screen's edge brings the room back.

Effortless Undo

A purpose-built history remembers your work in natural steps and stays instant even across a book-length manuscript, so no edit is ever a one-way door.

Always in View

A Writer's Dashboard

A quiet status line keeps a live count of words and characters, pages, words written today and this session, and progress toward the day's goal — select text and the counts scope to the selection.

A Day's Goal

Set a target in words or in pages and watch the day's work accumulate toward it, rather than guess at it.

Saved Without Thinking

Your file is saved continuously as you write; there is no command to remember and nothing to lose to a forgotten keystroke.

The Quiet Helpers

Tended as You Write

Sentences open with capitals, quotation marks curl correctly, a true em dash replaces the double hyphen, and an ellipsis forms from three dots — the hundred small chores of clean typography, done the instant before you'd notice.

A Watchful Eye

Spelling is checked as you go and common stumbles corrected from a list you can extend, while a guard leaves your rare or invented words untouched. Every helper switches off in a moment if you'd rather write unassisted.

Closed Pairs

Open a bracket or a quotation mark and its partner appears, with the cursor waiting between them.

Shaping the Prose

Emphasis & Structure

Bold and italics, headings at every level, blockquotes, and bulleted or numbered lists are each a single gesture from the keyboard or a right-click — and the page stays clean text underneath.

Illuminated Openings

Begin a chapter with a grand drop capital that sinks into the lines beneath, or a raised initial — the flourish of the old printed books, carried through to the finished export.

Collect & Separate

Collect draws a run of scattered lines into a single paragraph; Separate breaks a dense block back into one sentence per line for close revision.

Your Library

A Shelf at Your Side

Every chapter, scene, note, and fragment lives in a tidy tree along the edge of the room — created, renamed, duplicated, reordered, sorted, and dragged between folders in place. Hover any file to read its opening lines without opening it.

Find Everything, Replace Anything

Search the whole project at once and see every file a word appears in, with a count for each. Rename a character across every file in one move — after a preview of how many changes, with a recoverable copy of each file taken first.

A Living Table of Contents

An outline of the current chapter's headings stands ready beside the page, with drag-and-drop that moves whole sections — heading and prose together. Restructure a manuscript by dragging its table of contents.

Open Where You Belong

Reach any file in your system's own file manager in a click, and reopen Lectern to find your last directory, last file, and expanded folders exactly as you left them.

Make It Yours

Choose Your Light

Three crafted themes — warm Gold Elegance, cool Dark City, and soft Tea & Bamboo — reshape the whole room: paper, ink, accents, and the light around the page.

A Library of Type

Seven typefaces — Georgia, Times, Palatino, Garamond, Bookman, a clean sans, and Courier — each set to the size, color, and spacing you prefer.

Set the Room

Page width, margins, line spacing, paragraph indent and spacing, caret color, the page's shadow, the glow of a selection — every measure bends to your hand until the page reads exactly as you like.

Your Own Keys

Every shortcut is listed and yours to reassign, so the controls fit the hands that have always used them.

Peace of Mind

Nothing Is Ever Lost

Before every save, Lectern keeps a quiet snapshot of the previous version, holding as many as you choose, so any wrong turn is recoverable.

Your Work, in Your Keeping

Fold the whole project — or only its writing — into a single archive whenever you wish, carry your settings between machines, and update in place. Your settings travel without your API key, and your files never leave your disk.

Lectern Pro
☙❧

For when the work goes deeper — a worldbuilder's memory, an AI that writes in your voice, and a clean path to the finished manuscript.

The Compendium

A Book of Your World

Keep a private reference of your story's people, places, and lore — each entry named, with any number of aliases and tags. Name a character once, with every name they answer to, and a whole series can share one Compendium so the world stays consistent from book to book.

Woven In Without a Word

When the AI writes, it recognizes the names and aliases in your prose and draws the matching entries in automatically — so the details it produces stay true to the world you've built, with nothing to link by hand.

Artificial Intelligence, in Your Voice

When the Page Stalls

An AI assistant waits at a keystroke. Draft the next beat from a one-line note, continue a passage in flow, copy-edit a clumsy line, or rephrase a sentence — and define your own custom processes besides. The text streams onto the page in your voice; you bring your own key and choose any model, so the intelligence is yours to direct and the cost yours to control.

Tuned to Your Book

Set the tense, point of view, focal character, and a prose style guide once, and every suggestion is shaped to your novel's voice. Each process carries its own model, its own output color, and the choice to replace your text or write alongside it.

The Right Word

Select a word and call up a thesaurus drawn in context — not a flat list, but fresher choices that fit the sentence you're actually writing.

Highlight & Whiteout

A more elegant pair than underline and strikethrough. Highlight your foreshadowing so the AI knows where you're heading; Whiteout a passage to keep it from the AI until you decide whether it's worthy. On export, Highlight's color falls away and Whiteout passages are left behind entirely.

Sending It Into the World

A Polished Manuscript

Export to a fully styled Word document — headings, emphasis, blockquotes, lists, and your illuminated capitals all intact, set in the typeface and spacing you chose, with stray reference links quietly removed so only the story reaches the page.

Bound Together

Compile an entire folder of chapters into one Word document, or merge scattered scene files into a single manuscript in whatever order you set — the long work of assembling a draft, done in a single step.

A Study of Your Own Design

Every Shade Yours

Beyond the three themes, tune the color and style of every element — page, accents, dividers, the spellcheck mark, the glow on a recognized name — then save your design and carry it, with your custom AI processes, to any desk.
