Contribute

Help shape Lectern.

Lectern is a young project, built by writers for writers. Three ways to lend a hand — and one of them earns you Pro.

Make a video, earn Pro

Record your own short walkthrough of Lectern's free tier — the app actually running on your screen, showing the features you use — and we'll send you a Lectern Pro key, free. The video should run at least three minutes and show the product in action, not just a talking head or slides.

Email the link to submissions@necropolispublishing.com. We'll watch it and reply to that same address with your key.

☙❧

Found a glitch?

Lectern is young, and we appreciate all the help we can get. If something misbehaves, send it to glitch@necropolispublishing.com — tell us what happened and how to trigger it. If we can reproduce it, we'll fix it.

☙❧

Buy us a coffee

Lectern is a one-time purchase — no subscriptions, no tracking, no cloud. If it's earned a place at your desk and you'd like to help fuel the work, a coffee goes a long way.
